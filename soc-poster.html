01
What a SOC is

A Security Operations Center — the team + workflow + tooling that watches an organization's systems for attacks and drives the response. It brings people, process & technology together in one place.

The nerve center of defense — often staffed 24/7, because attacks don't keep office hours.

02
What it does
👁️
Monitor & detect — ingest telemetry, spot suspicious activity.
🔎
Investigate — is this real? how far did it get?
🛡️
Respond & recover — contain, eradicate, restore.

And keep improving the detections so the next attack is caught sooner.

03
The SOC loop — monitor → triage → investigate → respond → improve, and repeat

A continuous loop, not a one-off — every incident sharpens the detections that catch the next one.

04
The tiers & roles — alerts escalate up; expertise deepens

🟦 Tier 1 · Triage

Front line — works the alert queue, filters noise, escalates the real ones.

🟩 Tier 2 · Investigate

Digs into escalated incidents — scopes impact, runs the response.

🟧 Tier 3 · Hunt & IR

Senior analysts — proactive threat hunting & deep incident response.

Detection Engineer — builds & tunes the detections SOC Manager — runs the team, metrics & on-call
05
The stack the SOC runs on — collect → detect → automate → manage
06
Detection engineering

Turn attacker behavior (MITRE ATT&CK) + threat intel into tuned detections — codified, tested, version-controlled.

behavior→ detection rule→ tune→ fewer false positives

A precise detection is worth more than ten noisy ones.

07
Threat hunting

Proactively search for what the alerts missed — start from a hypothesis and assume breach, don't just work the queue.

🎯
Hypothesis-driven — "if an attacker were here, what would I see?"
🔁
Finds → detections — a good hunt becomes tomorrow's alert.

10
Watch out — the failure modes that quietly break a SOC
😵
Alert fatigue & burnout — the #1 SOC problem: too many alerts, tired analysts miss the real one.
🎛️
Untuned detections — noise drowns signal. Tune relentlessly for precision.
🤖
Manual toil — automate the repetitive steps (SOAR) so people do the thinking.
🕳️
Logging gaps — what you don't log, you can't see. Coverage is a prerequisite.
11
The takeaway
A SOC turns telemetry into detection & response through people, process & tooling — and is judged by how fast it detects and responds (MTTD / MTTR).
Collect telemetry→ Detect→ Respond→ Improve detections
